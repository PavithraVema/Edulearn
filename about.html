Our Story

EduLearn was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to everyone, everywhere. What started as a small team of passionate educators and technologists has grown into a global platform serving students across the world.

Our journey began when our founders, experienced educators themselves, recognized the barriers many faced in accessing quality education. They envisioned a platform where anyone with an internet connection could learn from the world's best instructors at an affordable price.

Today, EduLearn hosts thousands of courses across dozens of categories, from programming and business to personal development and the arts. We've helped over 1 million students gain new skills, advance their careers, and pursue their passions.
